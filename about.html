ABOUT ME
My name is Laila Mariel R. Enarne, a 20-year-old second-year student pursuing a Bachelor of Science in Information Technology at West Visayas State University, Lambunao Campus. I consider myself a cheerful and optimistic individual with a strong interest in the intersection of technology and creativity.

Although I do not consider myself inherently “creative”, I have a deep appreciation for the arts and artistic expressions. I find great joy in engaging with creative, aesthetic, and artistic works, and I have developed a passion for incorporating this love for design into my technical projects.

This website showcases my creations within platform technologies, where I combine my technical expertise in information technology with my enthusiasm for design and creativity. Each project is developed with dedication, attention to detail, and a genuine love for creating meaningful, functional, and aesthetically pleasing solutions.
